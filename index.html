ABOUT

I'm a data scientist and AI engineer. I build production LLM agent systems and bring AI into real products — grounding language models in a large structured knowledge graph so the answers they produce are accurate and verifiable. I care about systems that are reliable where it counts and genuinely useful to the people using them. Outside of work I build small developer tools and tinker with ML side projects.
